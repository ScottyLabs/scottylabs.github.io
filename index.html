About



ScottyLabs is a technical club at Carnegie Mellon University. We organize educational events to help people learn how to make things, host workshops to give students the opportunity to work on projects outside of class, and develop applications and services for the campus community.

Meetings

Our general body meetings are on Wednesdays. Come say hi and get involved! For exact times and locations, check out our calendar below.

If you wanna join tech, or just hang out with us, we have weekly hack sessions, Saturdays from 6pm to midnight in UC McKenna! Double check with calendar to see if we've canceled for that week, but otherwise come hang out, work on homework, or get involved with some of our awesome tech projects!
